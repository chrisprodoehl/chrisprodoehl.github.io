Demo Reel

Everything in the reel is my own work and made from scratch, from modeling and texturing to rigging and animating.

I did some procedural modeling in Houdini. The characters were almost all sculpted in Blender, with two exceptions:

  • In the man and fly shot, I made the human model and its base texture in MakeHuman and then did more painting in Substance Painter.
  • In the shots with the runners, the runners are one of Houdini's stock bipeds, their running animation one of the stock .bclips.

Texturing throughout with Substance Painter. Rendered mostly with Octane, but in some cases with Renderman or Mantra.

Realtime

Screen Recording. The demo was written with OpenGL and uses the Csound API. It's harder to tell without interacting with the program, but the pitch of the sound is sensitive to the height of camera view. The terrain and the clouds are generated from noise maps or functions. The whole thing is rendered via raymarching.

Screen Recording. OpenGL and Csound. The particle system is responsive to the audio signal Csound generates. The particles are solved in a compute shader. The audio could go on indefinitely (it's "algorithmic" in basically the most boring sense).
